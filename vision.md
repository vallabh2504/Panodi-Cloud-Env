# Vision: OmniLog (Anti-Tool-Fatigue)

## The Problem
Based on recent 2026 discussions on Reddit and X, a massive pain point for creators, builders, and indie hackers is "Tool Fatigue" and "Idea Paralysis." People are constantly switching between 10+ productivity tools (Notion, Jira, Trello, Apple Notes, GitHub Issues) and end up abandoning 90% of them because of friction in capturing quick thoughts.

## The Solution
**OmniLog** - The frictionless "Inbox Zero" for the mind. A highly opinionated, single-input web app that does one thing: captures thoughts, tasks, and ideas in under 2 seconds. No folders, no categories, no tags upon entry. Just dump it, and the system intelligently routes or stores it linearly. 

## Target Audience
Indie hackers, startup founders, and knowledge workers suffering from app overload.

## Value Proposition
- "Stop organizing, start capturing."
- Capture at the speed of thought without context switching.
