# Project: DelusionCheck.ai (The "Main Characters" Reality Check) 🤡📈

## 🚀 Concept
A viral, silly "Reality Check" tool for Instagram/TikTok creators. Users enter their "Goal" (e.g., "Become a billionaire by selling chai") and their "Current Reality" (e.g., "I spend 8 hours on Instagram"). The app uses AI (simulated via local logic/predefined roasts for Phase 1) to give them a "Delusion Score" and a brutal, sarcastic Hyderabadi roast.

## 🎨 Aesthetics (The "Exquisite" Standard)
- **Theme**: Cyber-Punk Sarcastic.
- **Vibe**: Dark mode, Neon Green/Pink accents.
- **Juice**: 
    - Floating 3D emojis (🤡, 💸, 📉).
    - Glowing progress bars for the "Delusion Meter".
    - Framer Motion "Pop" animations for the roast reveal.

## 🛠️ Features (MVP - Phase 1)
1. **The Input**: Two text areas for "Dream" vs "Reality".
2. **The Meter**: A custom SVG-based radial gauge that spins wildly.
3. **The Roast**: A randomly generated but context-aware brutal roast (Hyderabadi style: "Arrey mowa, chill...").
4. **Shareability**: A "Download Roast Card" or "Share to Story" mock-button.

## 🏗️ Technical Stack (For Claude-Worker)
- **Framework**: Vite + React 19 + Tailwind v4.
- **Animation**: Framer Motion.
- **Icons**: Lucide React.
